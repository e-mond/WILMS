**Base:** `feature/p14.3b-phase-4c1-schema`  
**Head:** `feature/p14.3b-phase-4c2-services`

---

## Executive Summary

Implements the **reconciliation business layer** for collector cash reconciliation MVP: domain formulas, repositories, transactional submit service with idempotency and audit. **No HTTP routes or frontend changes.**

---

## Business Purpose

At end of day, collectors submit physical cash counts. The service computes expected due (schedule-based), system recorded (CONFIRMED payments), classifies variance, persists an immutable submission with append-only history, and records audit events ÔÇö per approved 4B architecture.

## Architecture Alignment

- ExpectedCashFormula v1 ÔÇö schedule-based expected due

- Primary variance = physical ÔêÆ expected
- Duplicate prevention via lookup + DB unique index
- `RECONCILIATION_SUBMIT` idempotency scope
